About Me

Not only have I played many weddings, but I am also a member of the Main Street Symphony Orchestra in Duluth, GA. In the past I have played with different quartets, most recently the Main Street Quartet, also out of Duluth.

Specialties:Tension free playing is my biggest goal during any lesson. I suffered a neck injury because I played with tension and it was never addressed until it was almost too late. Now, after being rehabilitated, I pay much attention to the student, their posture, and any discomfort they are having so that they do not suffer how I had suffered.
