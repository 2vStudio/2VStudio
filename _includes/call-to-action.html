About Me

My name is Ginger Howard and I am accepting students of all ages in my violin/viola studio, which meets in my home. Each lesson is a private one-on-one session that covers many areas of the violin or viola (which ever instrument you decide upon).

Tension free playing is my biggest goal for my students. I suffered a neck injury because I played with tension and it was never addressed until it was almost too late. Now, after being rehabilitated, I pay close attention to the student, their posture, and any discomfort they are having so that they do not suffer how I had suffered. I focus on the ability to play long-term, whether that be for a future career or a life long hobby.

My studio also emphasizes on theory, history and music listening.

I am currently playing with The Forsyth Philharmonic and have also been involved with the Main Street Symphony Orchestra and Main Street Quartet.
